## For Donors

### Who can donate food?
Any food business — restaurants, bakeries, supermarkets, caterers, and event venues — can register as a Donor and list surplus food.

### What types of food can I list?
Packaged, prepared, or fresh food that is safe for human consumption and within its use-by date. We do not accept food that has already spoiled.

### How does the pickup work?
After you list a donation, NGOs in your area can claim it. A volunteer courier will coordinate the pickup directly with you via the app.

---

## For NGOs

### How do we receive food?
Once registered and verified, your NGO can browse available listings on the dashboard, claim items atomically, and schedule a volunteer pickup.

### Is there a cost?
Food Bridge is free for registered NGOs. Our platform is funded by partnerships and grants.

### How long does verification take?
Admin verification typically takes 1–2 business days after you upload your proof of establishment.

---

## For Volunteers

### Do I need a vehicle?
Not necessarily. Many donations are small enough to be carried on foot or by bicycle. You choose tasks that match your transport capacity.

### How do I get paid?
Volunteer deliveries are unpaid contributions. We're building a reward system — stay tuned!

## General

### Is my data safe?
Yes. We use Supabase with Row Level Security, meaning your data is only accessible by you and authorized admins.

### How do I report an issue?
Use the in-app feedback button or email **support@foodbridge.app**.

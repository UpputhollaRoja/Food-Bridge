## Our Mission

Food Bridge exists to eliminate food waste by building a fast, transparent, and community-powered redistribution network. We believe that no edible food should go to landfill while people go hungry.

## The Problem We Solve

Every year, **1.3 billion tonnes** of food is wasted globally — enough to feed every hungry person on Earth. Meanwhile, food production accounts for roughly 26% of all greenhouse gas emissions. We target the last mile: surplus food that is perfectly edible but discarded due to logistics, appearance, or overproduction.

## How We Do It

- **AI Matching** — Our AI engine prioritizes listings by shelf-life, volume, and proximity to NGOs.
- **Real-time Logistics** — Volunteers see optimized pickup and drop-off routes in-app.
- **Full Transparency** — Every gram of food rescued is logged and traceable.

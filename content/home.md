## How It Works

Food Bridge connects food donors (supermarkets, restaurants, bakeries) with NGOs and community food banks using real-time AI matching. Volunteers pick up and deliver the surplus food.

## Environmental Impact

Food waste generates nearly 10% of global greenhouse gas emissions. For every 1 kg of food surplus redirected through Food Bridge, we avoid an estimated **2.5 kg of CO2e** carbon emissions.
